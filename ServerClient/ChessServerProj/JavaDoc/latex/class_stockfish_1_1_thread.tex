\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{class_stockfish_1_1_thread}{Thread}} class keeps together all the thread-\/related stuff. We use per-\/thread pawn and material hash tables so that once we get a pointer to an entry its life time is unlimited and we don\textquotesingle{}t have to care about someone changing the entry under our feet.
